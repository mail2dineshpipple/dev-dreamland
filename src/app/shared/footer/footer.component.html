Dreamland Travel is a smart OTA company seamlessly providing travelers with comprehensive information, pricing, availability, and booking options for flights, hotels, holiday packages, buses, and car rentals across a vast network of 5000 cities from bustling metropolises to remote rural areas worldwide. As your trusted online travel partner we serve as a complete tour planner offering a one-stop solution for all your travel needs.

Our mission is to establish the world's largest and most intelligent travel marketplace uniting a global community of travelers with the finest travel booking services and destination insights while delivering unmatched value to travel suppliers and businesses aiming to connect with our vast audience.

At Dreamland Travel, we cover every facet of your travel journey from assisting you in selecting the perfect airplane seat to reading firsthand reviews of hotels and curating memorable experiences at your destination. Our extensive portfolio caters to both leisure and business travelers accommodating various tastes and budgets from modest to luxurious. We connect the dots channeling travel demand from every corner of the globe to over 110,000 hotels and a plethora of airlines tour operators, car rental agencies, and destination service partners.

Our multi-language customer service center operates around the clock, 24/7, 365 days a year, ensuring that both business and leisure travelers make informed and cost-effective bookings via our user-friendly website and mobile support. Dreamland Travel offers real-time air ticket bookings at the most competitive rates. As one of the largest hotel consolidators, we offer room reservations in over 2,500 domestic hotels across 250 destinations worldwide, complemented by international partnerships with major consolidators for access to 90,000+ hotels globally, always guaranteeing the best real-time booking rates.

But what truly sets us apart is our commitment to personalized service. We understand that, amid the technological convenience, there's nothing quite like speaking to a human being. So, when you find yourself overwhelmed or unable to find what you need our team of dedicated travel experts is just a phone call away, ready to assist you in making your travel dreams a reality.

Dreamland Travel is built on a foundation of personal knowledge, experience and a warm friendly approach offering top-tier standards at realistic prices. Whether you book your air tickets online or seek our guidance over the phone, we are here to ensure you have an exceptional travel experience."
